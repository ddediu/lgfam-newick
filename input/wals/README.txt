
WALS Online data download
=========================

Data of WALS Online is published under the following license:
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/2.0/de/deed.en

It should be cited as

Dryer, Matthew S. & Haspelmath, Martin (eds.) 2013.
WALS Online (v2020.3) [Data set]. Zenodo.
https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7385533
(Available online at https://wals.info, Accessed on 2024-08-05.) 

We downloaded here the files codes.csv, languages.csv, parameters.csv and 
values.csv from the current version (5 August 2024) of WALS from
https://github.com/cldf-datasets/wals/tree/master/cldf
